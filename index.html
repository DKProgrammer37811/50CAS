What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive child life and psychosocial programs designed to support children, families, communities, and healthcare professionals.

🏥

Child Life Internship & Training

India's first certified child life internship — a full-year, hands-on clinical placement within Manipal Hospitals, leading to internationally recognised certification.

💚

Hospital Staff Wellness Workshop

Structured wellness and resilience programs for hospital staff — addressing burnout, compassion fatigue, and nurturing a healthier care environment.

🌍

Community Outreach Program

Taking child life services beyond hospital walls — into schools, communities, and underserved areas to promote child wellbeing and psychosocial health.

🧠

Support & Community for Psychologists

A dedicated peer network, mentorship, and CPD resources for psychologists working in pediatric and hospital settings across India.

🔬

Completed, Ongoing & Future Research

Contributing to the evidence base for child life practice in India — from completed clinical studies to ongoing research and future collaborations.

🤝

Counselling & Psychological Support

Providing accessible counselling and psychological support across populations — children, families, caregivers, and healthcare teams across the lifespan.

About Us

Child Life Services

Child Life Services support the emotional and developmental needs of children and families during hospitalisation — through play, preparation, and counselling.

  • 🤝Family-centred care that keeps parents at the heart of every interaction
  • 🎮Evidence-based play therapy to reduce fear, pain, and anxiety in children
  • 📜Internationally recognised certification upon program completion
  • 🌍Pioneering India's growing pediatric psychosocial care movement
